Singapore Chicken Rice

White rice, boiled chicken with special sauces, side dishes with cucumbers and tomatoes.

We always select high quality ingredients, grown in greenhouses that meet US standards. This is one of our most popular seasonal dishes. The dish is cooked by a chef from Singapore. The restaurant commits that the food will have a delicious taste, suitable for your taste.

Seasonal Fruit Salad

Fresh salad, seasonal fruits such as strawberries, blueberries, plumes and more.

Salads are made with fresh leaves and served with seasonal fruits that are imported daily. The dish with a cool taste will be suitable for customers with mild palate, suitable for vegetarians and is the perfect accompaniment.

Vietnamese Crab Noodle

Boiled white noodle, seasonal crabs, vegetables, pork.

A popular dish with Vietnamese people. The dish is enhanced by the quality of premium seasonal crab meat and safe pork. Delicious broth will surely satisfy customers.

Christmas Lucky Cake

Peanuts, flour, eggs with mistlotoe fruits and leaves.

Lucky cake has long been a popular Asian dish. Inside the cake contains good wishes for Christmas Eve. This is a great dish for family and friends meals on Christmas Eve. This is a limited menu item for Christmas Eve at our restaurant.

Fresh Fruit Dessert

Fresh seasonal fruits like durian, strawberries, blueberries, cherries, apples.

Dishes for customers who are not into fruit salad and prefer fresh fruits. We have this dishes in pre-order menu for you to eat after meals. We served it with cream, salt which can satisfy all the customers feeling. This is a perfect ending for a meal.
